Why Bagel Boyz?

What Makes Our Bagels Hit Different

There's a reason people drive across Monmouth County for our bagels.

Boiled the Right Way

Every bagel is boiled before baking, creating that signature crispy crust and chewy interior that New Jersey demands.

Fresh Every Morning

Our bakers start before sunrise so you get hot, fresh bagels the moment we open at 6 AM. Every. Single. Day.

Boar's Head Deli

We use only Boar's Head premium meats and cheeses for our deli sandwiches. No shortcuts, no substitutes.

Family Owned

Founded by Robert and Jessica Ploussas, Bagel Boyz is a family business built on passion and community.
